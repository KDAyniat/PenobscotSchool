Our History...

...for over 60 years the Penobscot Community Elementary School has been providing a "Quality Education for the Leaders of Tomorrow". Our community school running grades Pre-K through Grade 8 has always strived to be at the forefront of education and learning, with some of the finest teachers in the state of Maine.
