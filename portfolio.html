Our History...

...MadCouch, Inc. was started in 2003. When the co-founders, who created MadCouch, lent their expertise to a burgeoning web based business, helping them expand their customer base and generate new revenue streams. Creating a new niche in web based consulting, MadCouch began the quest to provide high level technology and web consulting services to established and early stage web based technology ventures. MadCouch has assisted such companies such as Violet Valley, Grails48, 9jacob, Vinceria, Canocal and Startaroo with their web based marketing and revenue generation programs.
